When I started this project I had no idea where I wanted it to go. I thought that I wanted to create a hopeful narrative about the future of silver until I found out that silver is not ethically mined. After doing some research and finding out that there is no ethical way to find and extract silver I decided to change the narrative. I changed my phrase from “the future is silver” to “the cost of silver” to better reflect this narrative. I also added bruised and dirty hands to the top of the page to give a glimpse at what workers go through to mine silver. We need silver to create sustainable methods of keeping the human race going, but at what cost?
